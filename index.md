Effective date: May 28, 2026

Dosely is a medication reminder, medication tracking, pharmacy lookup, and health organization app provided by Proofsoft.

This Privacy Policy explains how Dosely accesses, collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app. By using Dosely, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Process

Dosely may process the following types of information depending on how you use the app.

### Medication and Health-Related Information

You may choose to enter medication-related information, including:

- Medication names
- Dosage and strength
- Medication type
- Dose schedule and reminder times
- Start and end dates
- Intake history, including taken, missed, skipped, or upcoming doses
- Stock or quantity information
- Doctor notes
- Personal notes
- Medication instructions

This information is used to provide medication reminders, schedule tracking, history, reports, widgets, and related app features.

Dosely does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, emergency services, or professional healthcare recommendations. Always follow the instructions of your doctor, pharmacist, prescription label, or qualified healthcare provider.

### Profile and App Preference Information

Dosely may store app-related preferences such as:

- Profile name or display information you choose to enter
- Date of birth, if provided
- Avatar selection
- App language
- Notification settings
- Reminder preferences
- Premium access state
- App onboarding or legal notice acknowledgement

### Location Information

Dosely may request access to approximate or precise location only when you use location-based features, such as finding nearby on-duty pharmacies.

Location access is optional. If you deny location permission, you can still use medication tracking and manually search pharmacies by city or district where available.

Dosely does not use your location for advertising personalization.

### Notification and Alarm Permissions

Dosely may request notification, exact alarm, vibration, foreground service, and full-screen alarm-related permissions to provide medication reminders, alarm screens, and reminder actions.

These permissions are used only to deliver reminder functionality that you configure in the app.

### Files and Reports

If you export or share medication progress reports, Dosely may create local files such as PDF reports on your device. These files are created only when you use export or share features.

## 2. How We Use Information

Dosely uses information to:

- Store and display your medication list
- Schedule medication reminders and alarms
- Track medication intake history
- Show upcoming, missed, taken, and skipped medications
- Display medication statistics and calendar history
- Generate local reports
- Manage medication stock reminders
- Support home screen widgets
- Provide nearby or selected-area on-duty pharmacy results
- Manage premium features and subscription access
- Improve app reliability and functionality
- Comply with legal, safety, and platform requirements

Dosely does not sell your medication or health-related information.

Dosely does not use medication or health-related information for advertising personalization.

## 3. Local Storage

Most medication, reminder, note, stock, profile, and history data is stored locally on your device.

If you uninstall the app, clear app storage, or delete medication records inside the app, locally stored data may be removed from your device.

Some device or operating system services may affect storage, backup, or transfer behavior depending on your Android settings. Dosely is designed to limit backup of sensitive local medication data where supported.

## 5. Data Sharing

Dosely may share or make data available only in limited situations:

- With service providers needed to operate app features, such as billing, subscriptions, ads, pharmacy lookup, maps, or platform services
- When you choose to export, share, or send a report
- When required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request
- To protect the rights, safety, or security of users, the app, or others
- As part of app store, platform, or policy compliance processes

Dosely does not sell your personal or health-related information.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

Medication, profile, reminder, stock, note, and history data stored locally remains on your device until you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall Dosely.

You can delete local data by:

- Deleting medications or notes inside the app
- Clearing Dosely app storage from Android settings
- Uninstalling Dosely

Subscription and purchase records are managed by Google Play and RevenueCat according to their own retention policies.

Ad, diagnostic, and technical data processed by third-party services is retained according to those providers’ policies.

## 7. Permissions

Dosely may request the following permissions depending on device version and enabled features:

- Notifications: to send medication reminders
- Exact alarms: to trigger medication reminders at scheduled times
- Full-screen intent or alarm-related display behavior: to show medication alarm screens
- Vibration: to alert you for reminders
- Location: to find nearby on-duty pharmacies
- Internet and network access: to load ads, validate subscriptions, and fetch pharmacy data
- Boot completed: to restore reminders after device restart

You can manage permissions from Android system settings. Some features may not work fully if required permissions are denied.

## 8. Children and Sensitive Information

Dosely is intended for users who can responsibly manage medication reminders or for use with the assistance of a parent, guardian, caregiver, or healthcare professional.

Medication and health-related information can be sensitive. If you enter information about another person, you are responsible for ensuring you have the right to do so.

Dosely is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent or supervision.
